<p>A relationship between an activity and one of the following:<br/></p><p><ul><li>the outcome of another activity where the source activity does not occur unless the target activity outcome has occurred and all activities are part of the global library of activities;</li><br/></p><p><li>another activity where the source activity does not occur unless the target activity has occurred and all these activities are part of a global library of activities;</li><br/></p><p><li>a group of other criteria that may be composed of a mix of other activities, observation results and/or other groups.</li><br/></p><p></ul><br/></p><p>For example, only perform a certain lab test if drug X was administered. (target = another activity)<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, only perform a substance administration of drug X if the blood pressure was over some threshold number. (target = observation result from another activity that is an observation)<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, only perform a substance administration of drug Y if the blood pressure was over some threshold number and either the result of a certain lab test was positive or the subjects temperature was elevated, i.e. "(A and (B or C))".<br/></p>
