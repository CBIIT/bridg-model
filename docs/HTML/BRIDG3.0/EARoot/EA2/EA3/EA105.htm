<p>A relationship between a criterion group and an option that can satisfy it, either an activity, observation result or other criterion group, i.e. a relationship between a logical set of parenthesis and one of the options inside the parentheses, where the criterion group and its option are both part of a global library of activities.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, a pain management criterion group may be comprised of three options, one for substance administration of Tylenol, another for substance administration of aspirin, and a third for substance administration of ibuprofen.  The pain management criterion would be satisfied/accomplished with any one of these activities and would be associated to each of the three via a different DefinedCriterionGroupOptionRelationship.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE: This class helps represent an OR relationship between siblings in the same criterion group.<br/></p>
