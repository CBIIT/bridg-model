<p>An action plan and execution of a pre-clinical or clinical study in human beings in which individuals are assigned by an investigator based on a protocol to receive specific interventions. StudySubjects may receive diagnostic, therapeutic or other types of interventions. The assignment of the intervention may or may not be random. The individuals are then followed and biomedical and/or health outcomes are assessed.<br/></p>
