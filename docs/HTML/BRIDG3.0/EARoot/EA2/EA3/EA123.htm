<p>A defined activity at a global library level, outside the context of any particular study, that, as a reusable template, is an action of applying, dispensing or otherwise giving medications or other substances.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, administration of methotrexate as part of chemotherapy.<br/></p>
