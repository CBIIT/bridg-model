<p>An activity that is intended to occur at some point in the course of a particular study.  <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, pregnancy tests are planned for StudySubjects who are females of childbearing potential.  The pregnancy tests are part of the study and the test should be applied to StudySubjects with an actualIndicator of "N" and a sexCode of "F"..<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE:  A PlannedActivity may be a container of other activities and have a complex structure involving components, options and contingencies using the associated relationship classes.  This structure allows BRIDG 3.0 to represent concepts in previous versions of BRIDG such as StudyCells, StudySegments and StudySubjectEncounters.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE: A planned activity could also be thought of as an activity at a particular stage in the business process in which the activities occur, i.e., in the "planned" stage rather than the "scheduled" stage or the "performed" stage. An instance of a planned activity is not assigned to a particular StudySubject, but to a "kind of" StudySubject. <br/></p>

public ST
  blindedDescription		 Map:C3PR=PlannedStudyCell.blindedDescription
Map:TDM=TrialCell.blindedDescription
Notes: The textual representation of the planned activity from the point of view of a blinded subject participant (study subject or study investigator). <br /><br />For example, during the second treatment epoch of a study, Arms A and B are still blinded and Arm C is no longer blinded. So, Arm A and B must have identical blindedDescriptions.<br /><br />For example, in a study with 3 arms, Arm 1: standard vaccine given in three shots at 2 months, 5 months, and 12 months of age; Arm 2: new vaccine given in three shots at 2 months, 5 months, and 12 months of age; Arm 3: new vaccine given in two shots at 2 months and 5 months of age.  Subjects assigned to the third arm are unblinded at some point during 5 months and 12 months. By the time of the Third Shot Epoch, the "Arm 3/Third Shot" activity can be called by this, its unblinded name.  However, the "Arm 1/Third Shot" and "Arm2/Third Shot" activities still need blinded names. Both these activities would have the blinded name (something like) "3-shot Arm/Third Shot".
