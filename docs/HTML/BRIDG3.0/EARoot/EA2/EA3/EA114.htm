<p>A defined activity at a global library level, outside the context of any particular study, that, as a reusable template, is an action whose immediate and primary intention is the alteration of the physical condition of the StudySubject or ExperimentalUnit.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, procedures may involve the disruption of some body surface (e.g. an incision in a surgical procedure), conservative procedures such as reduction of a luxated joint, including physiotherapy such as chiropractic treatment, massage, balneotherapy, acupuncture, shiatsu, etc.<br/></p>
