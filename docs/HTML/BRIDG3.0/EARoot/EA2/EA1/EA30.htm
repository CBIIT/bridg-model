<p>Specifies the link between an adverse event causal assessment and the observation result evaluated as a possible cause.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, a diagnosis of diabetes may be evaluated as a cause of an adverse event.<br/></p>

Attribute Details
public REAL
  probability		 Map:AE=CausalAssessment.probability
Notes: A number representing the likelihood of the identified cause of an adverse event.<br /><br />For example, 60% likelihood that an adverse event was caused by the evaluated result of diabetes.
public CD
  uncertaintyCode		 Map:AE=CausalAssessment.certaintyCode
Notes: A coded value specifying whether and to what degree this evaluation or observation has been asserted to be in doubt in any way.   <br /><br />For example, for adverse event in respect to CTEP and FAET, values may include: unrelated, unlikely, possible, probable, definite.<br /><br />For example, for adverse events in respect to SDTM & E2B, values may include:  related, possibly related, unlikely related, not related.
