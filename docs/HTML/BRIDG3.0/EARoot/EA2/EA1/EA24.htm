<p>Any unfavorable and unintended sign, symptom, disease, or other medical occurrence with a temporal association with the use of a medical product, procedure or other therapy, or in conjunction with a research study, regardless of causal relationship. <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, death, back pain, headache, pulmonary embolism, heart attack.<br/></p>
