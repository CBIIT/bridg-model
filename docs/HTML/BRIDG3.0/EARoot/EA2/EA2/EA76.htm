<p>An organization that approves, monitors and reviews biomedical research to protect the rights, safety and welfare of the StudySubjects. This committee performs critical oversight functions for research conducted on human StudySubjects that are scientific, ethical, and regulatory.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>For example, Institutional Review Board (IRB), ethics committee, research ethics board, etc.<br/></p>
