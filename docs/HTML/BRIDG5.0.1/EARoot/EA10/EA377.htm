DEFINITION:<br/>A relationship between a composite activity and a component activity that comprises it, i.e. parent and child activities where all activities have occurred in the context of a particular study or experiment.<br/><br/>EXAMPLE(S):<br/>An unplanned substance administration occurs as part of a planned encounter.<br/><br/>A planned substance administration occurs as part of an encounter that is different than the one in which it was originally planned.<br/><br/>An unplanned encounter contains a number of activities.<br/><br/>OTHER NAME(S):<br/><br/>NOTE(S):<br/>Particularly relevant when one or both of the activities are not planned as the composition relationship can't be established using the normal PlannedCompositionRelationship or DefinedCompositionRelationship.<br/>

The PerformedCompositionRelationship class may only be used when there is no DefinedCompositionRelationship or PlannedCompositionRelationship between the objects that the source and target relationship instantiate (directly or indirectly).  I.e., if the composition relationship can reasonably be expressed in the defined or planned representation, it must be expressed there, not in the performed representation.<br/>
