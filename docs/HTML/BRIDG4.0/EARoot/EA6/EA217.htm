DEFINITION:<br /></p><p>A physical entity which is the primary unit of operational and/or administrative interest in a study. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>EXAMPLE(S):<br /></p><p>A person who is registered in a study as a recipient of an investigational product or as a control. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Individuals who are being screened for studies.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Individuals participating in observational or other studies.  <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>A pacemaker, a fuse that can be used in medical devices, a cow, a pen of pigs, or a tissue sample from a tissue bank.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>OTHER NAME(S):<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>NOTE(S):<br /></p><p>StudySubjects within a study are all of the same type.  An entity registered in a study is not part of another entity registered in the same study.<br /></p>
