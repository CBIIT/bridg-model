<p>DEFINITION:<br/></p><p>A result of a clinical observation, i.e. from an examination, test or direct observation performed on the experimental unit.  <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>EXAMPLE(S):<br/></p><p>The performed observation of "WBC count" would be associated to a clinical result called "WBC result" in which the results of the observation (7500 WBCs) would be stored.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>OTHER NAME(S):<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE(S):<br/></p><p>There is an association between the observation that has the original data (and the original units) and the observation in which the data elements have been converted into standardized units.  <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>If further use case are discovered it may be necessary to move the recursive relationship up to PerformedObservationResult.<br/></p>
