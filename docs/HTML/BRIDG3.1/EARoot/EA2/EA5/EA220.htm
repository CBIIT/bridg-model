Notes: DEFINITION:<br />The date and time span when this activity began and ended.<br /><br />EXAMPLE(S):<br />The date and time when a sample is taken from the subject. <br /><br />A dose of chemotherapy is given on June 12th starting at 9am and finishing at 12pm.<br /><br />OTHER NAME(S):<br /><br />NOTE(S):<br />Whether administrative or preparatory activities are included in this time frame is up to whoever is defining the activity - this time frame is all that matters when the activity occurred.<br /><br />The date range is an interval (IVL) data type, meaning it has a beginning (low) and end (high) values. It is also a datetime (TS.DATETIME) data type, meaning the beginning date and the end date can have uncertain values (e.g., a range). For example, if a subject was diagnosed with leukemia sometime in 2009 and that subject had a transplant (substance administration) sometime in 2010. We can surmise that the subject must have had a preparative regimen sometime between those times. Therefore the preparative regimen activity would have a beginning (low) date range of between 2009-01-01 and 2009-12-31; and an ending (high) date range of between 2010-01-01 and 2010-12-31. The precise data type mappings would be:<br />IVL<TS.DATETIME>.low.uncertainRange.low = 2009-01-01<br />IVL<TS.DATETIME>.low.uncertainRange.high = 2009-12-31<br />IVL<TS.DATETIME>.high.uncertainRange.low = 2010-01-01<br />IVL<TS.DATETIME>.high.uncertainRange.high = 2010-12-31<br /><br />[Note that times are left out of the example to keep it as simple as possible.]
