<p>DEFINITION:<br/></p><p>The possible or expected results that can be obtained by observing, monitoring, measuring or otherwise qualitatively or quantitatively recording one or more aspects of physiologic or psychologic processes.<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>EXAMPLE(S):<br/></p><p>The normal range for a systolic blood pressure reading is 110-130 for adult males:<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.referenceTypeCode = normal range<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.valueTypeCode = systolic blood pressure<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.value(ANY=>IVL<PQ>) = 110-130 mm[Hg]<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.populationScopeCode = adult males<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>The limit of quantitation for a PK assay, concentration of drug XYZ is 2-60 nanograms/ml for adult women as processed by Acme Labs:<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.referenceTypeCode = limit of quantitation<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.valueTypeCode = PK assay, concentration of drug XYZ<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.value(ANY=>IVL<PQ>) = 2-60 nanograms/ml<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult > PerformingLaboratory > Laboratory > Organization.name = Acme Labs<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>The normal range for a hemoglobin concentration measurement on an adult female performed by Acme Labs is 12.1 to 15.1 g/dL:<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.referenceTypeCode = normal range<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.valueTypeCode = hemoglobin concentration measurement<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.value(ANY=>IVL<PQ>) = 12.1 to 15.1 g/dL<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.populationScopeCode = adult females<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult > PerformingLaboratory > Laboratory > Organization.name = Acme Labs<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>The normal range for a fasting blood glucose measurement on an adult performed by Acme Labs is 3.6 to 5.8 mmol/L:<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.referenceTypeCode = normal range<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.valueTypeCode = blood glucose measurement<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.value(ANY=>IVL<PQ>)= 3.6 to 5.8 mmol/L <br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.populationScopeCode = adult<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult.fastingStatusIndicator = true<br/></p><p>ReferenceResult > PerformingLaboratory > Laboratory > Organization.name = Acme Labs<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>OTHER NAME(S):<br/></p><p>Normal range, reference range, limit of quantitation, clinical concern range, data checking range, alert range<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE(S):<br/></p><p>The context of a reference result may include any combination of the following:  the performing lab, the performing device, and the population to which the reference value pertains.  The context may also omit any of those aspects as well.<br/></p>
