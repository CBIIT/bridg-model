<p>DEFINITION: <br/></p><p>Any unfavorable and unintended sign, symptom, disease, or other medical occurrence with a temporal association with the use of a medical product, procedure or other therapy, or in conjunction with a research study, regardless of causal relationship. <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>EXAMPLE(S):<br/></p><p>death, back pain, headache, pulmonary embolism, heart attack<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>OTHER NAME(S):<br/></p><p><br/></p><p>NOTE(S):<br/></p><p>The BRIDG SCC has a GForge Tracker Issue (#31893) indicating a need to validate the requirement that every AdverseEvent be described by a SafetyReportVersion.<br/></p>
