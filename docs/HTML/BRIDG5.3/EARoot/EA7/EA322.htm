DEFINITION:<br/>A designation used to segregate study subjects into collections in order to balance the study for analysis. The stratum group is made up of a combination of stratification criterion answers, which ultimately is used to assign study subjects or experimental units to arms on a study.<br/><br/>EXAMPLE(S):<br/>A given study may want to require even distribution of gender and age so stratum groups may be defined as:  males under 18, males 18 and over, females under 18, and females 18 and over.<br/><br/>OTHER NAME(S):<br/><br/>NOTE(S):<br/>
