DEFINITION:<br /></p><p>Specifies the link between an observation result and the actions taken as a result of that outcome.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>EXAMPLE(S):<br /></p><p>An electrical problem (product investigation result) in a device could result in a recall (product action taken) of the device.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>An abnormal lab result triggers a follow-up lab test for further investingation.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Study dose reduced or protocol treatment change due to an adverse event.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>OTHER NAME(S):<br /></p><p>AdverseEventActionTakenRelationship, ProductActionTakenRelationship<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>NOTE(S): <br /></p><p>Action taken relationship is generally used for "unplanned" activities rather than those already determined in "contingent" relationships in the planned study design.<br /></p>
