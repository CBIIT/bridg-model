The Imaging sub-domain represents the core concepts related to this domain, including imaging protocols, imaging activities, images and other related concepts.<br/><br/><font color="#ff0000"><b>Collaboration with other Imaging work groups</b>: With the broader scope of BRIDG 4.0 covering translational research and the harmonization of Life Sciences Model and CDISC Pharmogenomic & Pharmocogenetics domains, it is likely that there is some overlap of concepts between BRIDG Imaging sub-domain concepts and the models being developed in the NCI Imaging Workspace community, HL7 Imaging work group, etc.  BRIDG recognizes these group models as peers or sibling models and is committed to working with the teams to align on common semantics.  The BRIDG team has started the conversation with the NCI Imaging community to this effect and plans to continue the dialogue on how to operationalize the collaboration and leverage the subject matter expertise of this and other groups.  </font><br/>
