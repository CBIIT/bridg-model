DEFINITION:<br/>The person, organization, or device that executes or accomplishes an activity.<br/><br/>EXAMPLE(S):<br/>surgeon, performing laboratory, monitoring device, healthcare provider, adjudication committee, family member, radiologist, vendor (may provide a uniform assessment for all sites participating in a study), heart rate monitor, pace maker.<br/><br/>OTHER NAME(S):Assessor<br/><br/>NOTE(S):A Performer may be simply a person, organization or device or it may be a person, organization or device in a particular role that is important for understanding or interpreting the significance of the activity or its results, such as with observations.<br/>
