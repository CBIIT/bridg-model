Attribute Details
public Decimal
  probability		 Range:0 to 1
Notes: The probability assigned to the value, a decimal number between 0 (impossible) and 1 (certain), inclusive. <br /><br />There is no "default probability" that one can assume when the probability is unstated. Therefore, it is impossible to make any semantic difference between a UVP without probability and a simple T. UVP does not mean "uncertain", and a simple T does not mean "certain". In fact, the probability of the UVP could be 0.999 or 1, which is quite certain, where a simple T value could be a very vague guess.
