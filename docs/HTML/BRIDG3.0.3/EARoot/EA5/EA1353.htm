<p>A part that may have a type-tag signifying its role in the address. Typical parts that exist in about every address are street, house number, or post box, postal code, city, country but other roles may be defined regionally, nationally, or on an enterprise level (e.g. in military addresses). <br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Addresses are usually broken up into lines, which may be indicated by special line-breaking delimiter elements (e.g., DEL). <br/></p>
